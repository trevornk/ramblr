Fixed the real cause of the Pixel Fold black-screen bug on fold/unfold (the v1.0.5 fix only addressed half of it). On-device log analysis pinned the true root cause to the small feedback bubble that pops up after dictation: it was still being hidden by removing its window content entirely, and one non-drawing overlay window is enough to stall the OS's own fold display-switch sync for a fixed ~2 seconds. The feedback bubble now hides the same safe way the main ring already did, so folding/unfolding (including while locked) is fast again.
