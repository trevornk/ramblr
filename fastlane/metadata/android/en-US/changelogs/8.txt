Fixed a real Pixel Fold black-screen bug on fold/unfold, root-caused via on-device log analysis: Ramblr's floating overlay window was being torn down and rebuilt (visibility=GONE) on the exact same screen-lock/fold events that drive the OS's own fold display-resize transition, causing WindowManager to lose track of the overlay window mid-transition and stall the screen for a couple of seconds -- worse (requiring two power-button presses) when the device was locked during the fold. The overlay now stays attached at all times and is hidden via transparency instead, and its fold-triggered repositioning no longer runs concurrently with the OS's own display transition.
